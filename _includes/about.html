About

I am passionate about brain science, and I have the experience to show for it!

 

In 2017 obtained a B.S. in Psychology from the University of Oklahoma, where I also worked as a research assistant in the OU Visual Neuroscience Lab (2016-2018), OU Memory Lab (2016), and Lab of Integegrated Neuroimaging and Neuromodulation (2017). In 2018 I accepted my current position as an Associate in Research at the Center for Cognitive Neuroscience at Duke University, where I work as a lab manager for the Cabeza Lab (2018-current) and research assistant for Cabeza Lab projects and others such as the Electric Dino Lab (2018-2021) and the Duke Motivated Aging and Cognition Lab (2021-current).

 

Writing is another passion of mine, and I am currently developing my skills as a science communicator! *
